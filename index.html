FAQ

Is this live streaming?

No. Megaverse Live offers live 1:1 coaching using video calls. You work directly with a tutor in real time — not recorded lessons.

Is this only IELTS?

No. We cover IELTS and TOEFL, plus speaking, writing, academic English, and university communication.

How many sessions do I need?

It depends on your deadline and starting level. Most students start with a trial, then choose a 4–8 session bundle.

Do you use AI?

We’ll add AI support for notes, practice prompts, and session summaries. Your learning strategy and scoring guidance come from a human tutor.
