FAQ

Is this live streaming?

No. Megaverse Live offers live 1:1 coaching using video calls. You work directly with the coach in real time — not recorded lessons.

Is this only IELTS?

No. We cover IELTS and General English (speaking & writing). Other offerings may be added later.

How do I confirm my session?

After you submit the booking form, you’ll receive a secure payment link to confirm your slot.

How many sessions do I need?

It depends on your timeline and current level. Most students start with a trial, then choose a 4–8 session bundle.
