WHAT TO DO IN NSONE:
====================

1. Log into NSOne: https://nsone.net
2. Find domain: megaverselive.com
3. Add CNAME record with these values:

   Record Type: CNAME
   Record Name: @ (or leave blank for root)
   Record Value: megaverse-live.onrender.com
   TTL: 3600 (default)

4. For www subdomain (optional):
   Record Name: www
   Record Value: megaverse-live.onrender.com

5. Click Save/Create

WAIT FOR DNS TO PROPAGATE (10-15 minutes)

VERIFY IT WORKS:
================

1. Open browser
2. Visit: https://megaverselive.com/
3. Should see your booking platform ✓

If not working after 15 minutes:
- Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+F5 (Windows)
- Check DNS propagation: https://www.whatsmydns.net/?d=megaverselive.com
