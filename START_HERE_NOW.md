## 🎯 What Happens When You Deploy

1. **Users book sessions** → Go to your website
2. **Payment processing** → Razorpay (UPI for India) or PayPal (international)
3. **Booking confirmed** → Data saved to database
4. **Calendar updated** → Your calendar tool sends confirmation email
5. **You get paid** → Straight to your account
